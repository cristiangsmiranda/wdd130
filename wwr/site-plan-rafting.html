Overview

Purpose

Our aim is to provide a challenging, exciting and fun family experience. Rafting is the ideal choice for those who love fun and team challenges!

Audience

Tired of family activities, or friends with no excitement?

Rafting is the ideal option for your new adventure.

Everyone can enjoy being in touch with nature and develop teamwork

by overcoming obstacles together while having fun with every paddle.

How many people can take part in the water rafting trail?

Depending on the raft used, 4 to 12 people can go together in the same raft

What is the minimum and maximum age allowed?

With different routes designed in levels, as well as high-quality safety equipment,

children, young people, adults and the elderly can take part, with smaller trails for children

and the elderly and larger trails for young people and adults (from 6 years old to 75 years old).
